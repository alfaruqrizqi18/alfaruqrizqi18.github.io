The Best Pineapple Supplier

Pineapple Hut Indonesia is a part of PT. Sennit Global Indonesia. Pineapple Hut Indonesia is a supplier of the highest quality pineapple fruit from Indonesia. We can help your pineapple-related business become bigger and better known for its taste and quality.

Our vision and mission is to be a supplier of the highest quality pineapple fruit to all corners of the world. The land used as a medium for growing pineapple fruit from Indonesia's Pineapple Hut is soil that has been mixed with ash from the eruption from Mount Kelud. Apart from good quality and taste, plants are also fertile. Pineapple from Pineapple Hut Indonesia has a sweet taste, abundant water content and also has a large size. The pineapples we provide are very suitable to help your business such as canned fruit, jam and others.
